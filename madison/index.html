Finding ant nests inside or outside your home, or discovering ant activity in unexpected areas are clear indicators. These tiny invaders can be incredibly persistent, seemingly appearing out of nowhere and multiplying rapidly. They can contaminate food, and certain species can deliver painful bites. Dealing with ants effectively requires identifying the species and locating the colony, which can be challenging for the untrained eye.
